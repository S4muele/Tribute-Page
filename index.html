A timeline of Hubble life

  • 1889 - Born Edwin Powell Hubble in Marshfield, Missouri.
  • 1910 - Obtains a Bachelor of Science from the University of Chicago.
  • 1917 - Obtains a PhD from the University of Chicago.
  • 1924 - The New York Times publishes his findings.
  • 1929 - Studies the redshift of distant galaxies.
  • 1941 - Reports to the American Association for the Advancement of Science.
  • 1949 - Dies while on vacation in Colorado.
